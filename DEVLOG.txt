[M3l] 2026-07-08 - Standalone settle race fix + horseshoe spacing
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- M3k's layout CONFIRMED WORKING on the iPhone (screenshot: parchment
  status strip above, nav clear of the home bar) but ~1 in 3 cold
  standalone launches ended shifted down+right until relaunch. Cause:
  iOS settles the safe-area insets at an unpredictable moment in the
  first seconds, with NO event; our single 350ms scale.refresh()
  could land mid-settle and stick. Now refreshing on a spread of

  events - the last refresh after settling wins.

  44px glyphs); 5/5 text follows.

[M3k] 2026-07-08 - HOTFIX: standalone safe-area take 2 (M3j failed)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- M3j's margin-top + calc(100dvh - env(...)) approach FAILED on the
  user's iPhone: top bar still under the Dynamic Island, bottom nav
  pushed up AND clipped. Diagnosis: the calc()+env() height line
  didn't take (Safari parser quirk) while margin-top did - container
  kept full height, got pushed down, overflow clipped the bottom.

  1) apple-mobile-web-app-status-bar-style black-translucent ->
     "default": standalone webview now STARTS BELOW the status bar /
     island (iOS paints that strip with our parchment theme-color).
  2) #game is position:fixed with top/bottom: env(safe-area-inset-*)
     - a plain inset box, no height computation at all. Browser
     Safari resolves the insets to 0 (unchanged behavior).
  3) main.ts: game.scale.refresh() on resize/orientationchange/
     visualViewport resize + once 350ms after boot - standalone can
     settle insets after our first measurement without any event.
     Aspect drift just letterboxes into parchment.
- Xcode guidance given: simulator/Capacitor wrap is the ONLY true
  test of native inset behavior, but not needed for the web loop yet;
  do a first Capacitor wrap dry-run (~M5) if web-standalone quirks
  keep burning playtest cycles; full treatment stays at M10.
