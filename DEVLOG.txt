[M1] 2026-07-05 - Lasso capture prototype
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Built the full Pokemon Ranger-style capture mini-game (CaptureScene):
  loop drawing, rope budget (resets per loop), banked-loop pips,
  body-touch line break (no damage), telegraphed radial attacks that
  break the line AND damage GRIT (lasso HP), release-mid-draw stun with
  anti-cheese (no progress during stun, creature gets faster after).
- Pure-math capture modules: geometry.ts (segment intersection,
  point-in-polygon, point-to-segment), LassoLine.ts, CreatureActor.ts
  (graze / flee / charge movement AI + attack telegraph state machine).
- 3 placeholder targets in src/data/species.ts: BESSIE cow (graze, 2
  loops), PECKSY hen (flee, 3 loops), DUSTDEVIL (charge + radial burst,
  4 loops, 25 dmg). Sprites are runtime-generated placeholder graphics.
- Scene flow: Boot (texture gen + Silkscreen font load) -> Title ->
  CaptureSelect -> Capture. Shared button helper in src/ui/button.ts.
- Docs: docs/GAME_DESIGN.md (full concept), docs/ROADMAP.md (M0-M10).
- Renamed README.md -> GUIDE.md (user keeps their own README on GitHub).
- STATUS: awaiting first playtest feedback (loop feel, rope budget 1600,
  Dustdevil fairness). Tuning knobs: top of CaptureScene.ts + species.ts.
